Turks and Caicos

Photographs and favorite parts of the Islands by Madison.

Beaches

One of the best parts about Turks and Caicos are the many beautiful white sand beaches. Here is a picture of the beach and clear blue water view we had from the balcony of the home we stayed in.

Iguanas

Little Water Cay, also known as Iguana Island is home to rock iguanas. The cay has a small tour dock, welcome center, and nature paths, and is a popular spot on boat charters operating the eastern side of Providenciales. This is a picture of an iguana we saw when visiting the Island.

Tourist Attractions

The La Famille Express is the rusty ship pictured above. It was built in 1953 and was origianlly an oil rig service ship. The ship was firmly planted in the shallow waters of the Caicos Banks during Hurricane Frances in 2004. While on vacation, my family and I were able to climb aboard and jump from the top deck.
